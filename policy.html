Privacy Policy & Terms of Service

ZooMinder
Last updated: December 18, 2025

1. Introduction

ZooMinder ("we", "our", "the app") is a subscription-based mobile application designed to help pet owners manage their pet profiles and medication schedules. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information.

2. App Functionality

Pet Management

Our application allows you to:

  • Register pet profiles: Store information about your pets including name, species, breed, age, and photos
  • Track medications: Manage medication schedules, dosages, and frequencies for each pet
  • Set reminders: Receive notifications for medication times
  • Cloud synchronization: Securely sync your data across devices (Premium only)

Secure Cloud Storage Encrypted

  • Encrypted data: All your data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Account-based access: Your data is protected by secure authentication
  • Cross-device sync: Access your pet information from any device

3. Information We Collect

ZooMinder collects and stores the following information:

  • Account information: Email address for authentication and account recovery
  • Pet information: Name, species, breed, age, and optional photos
  • Medication data: Medication names, dosages, frequencies, and schedules
  • Subscription data: Subscription status and billing information (processed by app stores)
  • Usage data: App usage patterns to improve our service and detect abuse

4. How We Use Information

The information collected is used to:

  • Provide and maintain the ZooMinder service
  • Sync your pet profiles and medication schedules across devices
  • Send medication reminders and notifications
  • Process and manage your subscription
  • Detect and prevent abuse of the service
  • Improve our app and user experience

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Secure cloud storage: Data is stored on secure, encrypted servers
  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Authentication: Protected by secure authentication protocols
  • No third-party sharing: We never sell or share your personal data with third parties
  • Data retention: Your data is retained while your account is active and deleted upon request

6. Permissions

ZooMinder may request the following device permissions:

  • Camera/Photo Library: To add photos of your pets (optional)
  • Notifications: To send medication reminders (optional)
  • Internet: To sync data and process subscriptions

8. Billing & Cancellation

  • Subscription billing: Premium subscriptions are billed monthly through Google Play
  • Auto-renewal: Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period
  • Cancellation: You may cancel your subscription at any time through Google Play settings
  • Refunds: Refund requests are handled according to Google Play policies
  • Downgrade: Upon cancellation, your account reverts to the Free plan at the end of the billing period

9. Acceptable Use Policy

To ensure a fair experience for all users, the following usage limits and policies apply:

Abuse Prevention

We reserve the right to suspend or terminate accounts that:

  • Attempt to circumvent subscription limits through multiple accounts
  • Use automated tools or bots to interact with the service
  • Share account credentials with others
  • Engage in any activity that disrupts or burdens our infrastructure
  • Violate these Terms of Service

Fair Use Policy: Premium accounts include unlimited pets and medications. However, accounts with more than 50 pets or more than 500 total medications may be subject to verification to ensure compliance with our terms and prevent automated or commercial abuse. If you have legitimate professional use requiring these higher volumes (veterinary clinics, pet care facilities, etc.), please contact us at inf.carlos@outlook.com to verify your account.

Account Responsibility: You are responsible for maintaining the security of your account credentials and for all activities under your account.

10. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: View all your data directly in the app
  • Modify: Edit or update any information at any time
  • Delete: Remove any pet profile or medication record
  • Export: Request a copy of your data
  • Account deletion: Request complete deletion of your account and all associated data

Account Deletion

You have the right to request permanent deletion of your account at any time. To delete your account:

  • Send an email to inf.carlos@outlook.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request"
  • Include your registered email address in the request
  • Your account and all associated data (pets, medications, history) will be permanently deleted within 30 days
  • This action is irreversible and cannot be undone
  • If you have an active subscription, it will be cancelled automatically
